Ohio Fleet Sprinter Van Warranty Options

In Ohio, commercial fleets requiring Sprinter vans often search for comprehensive warranty coverage to protect their investment. The state has several options for fleets to consider when selecting an extended warranty, with varying levels of coverage and costs.

Here are some key considerations for fleets in Ohio looking for a reliable extended warranty for their Sprinter vans:

  • Level of coverage: Determine the extent of coverage needed, considering the age and condition of the vehicles in the fleet. Comprehensive warranties often include repairs for parts and labor, while maintenance and inspection costs may not be included.
  • Rebates and incentives: Some manufacturers and repair shops offer rebates and incentives, such as reduced costs for annual inspections. These can help offset the cost of extended warranty coverage.
  • Manufacturer support: Choose a warranty provider that offers manufacturer support for the Sprinter vans, ensuring consistent maintenance quality and timely repairs.
  • Deductible and copay: Review and compare the deductible and copay amounts, considering the cost implications for the fleet.
  • Mileage and term coverage: Select a warranty that matches the typical lifespan and mileage requirements of the Sprinter vans in the fleet, avoiding unnecessary costs at the end of the warranty period.
  • Warranty transferability: If the vans will be sold or leased, choose a warranty that is transferable to new owners.
  • Cost: Compare warranty prices among providers, taking into account the level of coverage and benefits included.

Top Commercial Fleet Warranty Options for Sprinter Vans in Ohio:

The following are some leading commercial fleet warranty options for Sprinter vans in Ohio:

  • MagnaTrade Commercial: Offering comprehensive warranties for new and used Sprinter vans, MagnaTrade Commercial provides repair and maintenance coverage, as well as manufacturer support.
  • Priority One Warranty Solutions: This warranty provider offers customizable plans, including mileage-based and term-based options, providing protection for commercial fleets with varying vehicle lifespans.
  • AmeriWarranty: AmeriWarranty offers extended warranty solutions for Sprinter vans, including manufacturer-backed policies, providing repair and maintenance coverage for commercial fleets.
  • Auto Protection Group (APG): APG provides comprehensive warranty coverage, including protection for parts, labor, and maintenance, helping commercial fleets budget for long-term expenses.
