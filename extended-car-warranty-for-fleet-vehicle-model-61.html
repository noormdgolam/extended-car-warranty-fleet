Extended Car Warranty for Fleet Vehicle Model 61

The Extended Car Warranty for Fleet Vehicle Model 61 provides comprehensive coverage to business owners and fleet managers, ensuring that their vehicles operate optimally with minimal downtime. This extended warranty is specifically designed for the Model 61, taking into account its unique characteristics and performance requirements.

Key benefits of the Extended Car Warranty for Fleet Vehicle Model 61 include:

  • Cost savings: By transferring maintenance and repair costs to the warranty provider, business owners and fleet managers can expect significant cost savings over the extended warranty period.
  • Increased vehicle uptime: With comprehensive coverage, fleet managers can minimize vehicle downtime, ensuring that their vehicles remain on the road and generating revenue for their business.
  • Improved customer satisfaction: By providing a reliable and well-maintained fleet, business owners can enhance their reputation and build long-term customer relationships.
  • Extended lifespan of vehicles: The Extended Car Warranty for Fleet Vehicle Model 61 includes routine maintenance programs and repairs to extend the lifespan of vehicles, resulting in a lower total cost of ownership.

Vehicle Eligibility

The Extended Car Warranty for Fleet Vehicle Model 61 is available for vehicles with a minimum of 24 months or 30,000 miles (48,000 km) remaining on the manufacturer's warranty. Vehicles that have been heavily modified or used for commercial purposes may be eligible for the warranty, subject to review and approval by the warranty provider.

Coverage Terms and Conditions

Coverage under the Extended Car Warranty for Fleet Vehicle Model 61 begins on the date the warranty is purchased and ends at the expiration of the stated warranty term, unless the warranty is terminated early as per the warranty document.

Coverage includes repair or replacement of defective parts and labor for up to 5 years or 50,000 miles (80,500 km), whichever comes first. Routine maintenance programs and repairs are also covered, subject to the warranty provider's guidelines.

Exclusions and Limitations

The Extended Car Warranty for Fleet Vehicle Model 61 excludes coverage for damage caused by:

  • Accidents, theft, or vandalism.
  • Normal wear and tear, maintenance, and routine repairs.
  • Damage from neglect or misuse.

The warranty provider reserves the right to inspect vehicles and verify claims before providing repair or replacement coverage. A written estimate must be provided to the warranty provider before any repairs are made.
