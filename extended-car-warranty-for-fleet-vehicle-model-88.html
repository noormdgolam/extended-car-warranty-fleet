Benefits of Extended Car Warranty for Vehicle Model 88

Several benefits can be derived from extended car warranty coverage for the Vehicle Model 88, including:

Types of Extended Car Warranty Coverage Available

Fleet Vehicle Model 88 operators have access to two primary types of extended car warranty coverage:

  • Bumper-to-Bumper Warranty: Extends basic warranty coverage, which typically runs for the duration of the manufacturer's warranty.
  • Powertrain Warranty: Covers major engine and transmission components, often for longer than the standard warranty.
  • Comprehensive Warranty: Offers comprehensive coverage for most vehicle components, sometimes referred to as an all-inclusive or maximum coverage option.
